DJI MAVIC VS GOPRO KARMA

DJI MAVIC

GOPRO KARMA

The Gopro Karma and Dji Mavic are 2 of the most popular drones you can buy.
In My opinion I like the Dji Mavic more.
Although both the Dji Mavic and Gopro Karma have a foldable design,
The Dji mavic is a lot smaller and is easier to carry.
In saying that the Gopro Karma comes with a bag full of acsessories
like a stabiliser for the Hero 5 gopro camera it comes with.
In terms of the cameras, the Mavic has its own built in camera
which shoots at around 4k which is simalar to the Gopro Karma's camera
except the Gopro on the gopro karma comes off
and can basically be used like a normal gopro.
The Mavic also has tonnes of ultrasonic sensors placed all over the body of the drone
The sensors on the mavic stop it from crashing,
so if you are flying youre drone and loose control,
and you about to crash the drone into a tree,
it will take control and fly back to you.
Lastly, another feature on the Mavic is the ability to fly back to you when out of range,
this is a great feature if you are flying high and lose range with the drone
or if you are flying far out while watching the camera.
