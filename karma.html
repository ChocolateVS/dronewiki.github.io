___KARMA___

The karma like the MAVIC is a foldable drone, although the folded state is larger than the MAVIC it is still very easy to carry. The GOPRO KARMA comes with its own bag a detachable GOPRO hero 5 and a unique stabilising grib. The drone gimbal can be detached and inserted into the stabiliser grib creating a handheld electronic gimbal awesome! The drone con hover in the exact place due to its altitude hold and GPS postioning.This drone is designed for sports and extreame sports people or the occasional movie maker. The foldable drone and very own backpack makes it easy to take snowboarding, fishing, camping and get all the great shot you want to get. The controller itself has a very unique design wich fits in with the Karma's design, you dont need a cell phone as it come with a built in screen. but the screen is a little hard to se in a light enviroment.
