3DR SOLO VS PHANTOM4

PHANTOM4

3DR SOLO

The Phantom4 and 3dr solo are both very good drones
The Phantom4 has a longer flight time than the 3DR solo
at about 24 minutes for the phantom and 15 minutes for the Solo
This is because the Phantom 4 has a slightly more powerfull battery
and is lighter than the 3dr Solo
Both drones have a sensor system so that they wont crash into things
The 3dr solo is open source and all the code used to program the drone is avaible
This is a good feature if people want to build their own drones and things
Both drones have great cameras, The 3DR solo has a gopro hero 4 camera
In my opinion the hero 4 is a better camera because you are able to remove it
and use it as an everyday camera.
Both drones also have the feature where if they fly to far away they
they will return to you in the first place that they connected to the satellites
(Usually when you first turn them on before flight and put them on the ground)
Both of the drones have good apps which will tell you the
Altitude and position of the drone, and allow you to take photos/video within the app
