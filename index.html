What is a Drone?

A Drone is an unmanned aircraft, Here we are specifically talking about Quadcopers
which are drones usually with an X frame and 4 propellers.
These drones are controlled using radio transmitters.

There are 4 main types of Quadcopters

- Photography Quadcopters
- Toy Quadcopters
- Racing Quadcopters
- Delivery Quadopters

We will be focusing on photgraphy Quads
Photography Quads are the most epensive type of quadcopters
they are used for making movies and videos.
Photography Drones are equipped with a FPV (first person view)system
which by WIFI sends the video from the quad to the screen/phone attached to the controller
so you can get your shots right and dont need to to see the drone to be able to fly it.
